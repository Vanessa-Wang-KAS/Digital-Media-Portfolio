Description

This page was made for elementary Halloween. I choose orange as the background color as I wanted to match the Halloween theme. There is a banner that says Halloween on it, I figured it was a cute way of adding the title of the page without it looking too formal. The banner was hard to make since I have to draw the shapes by hand as they are all different. There are also some cute Halloween-related icons on different parts of the page to add liveliness.
